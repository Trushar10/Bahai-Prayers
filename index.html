Morning Prayer

As the sun rises and a new day begins, I come before You with a grateful heart. Thank You for the gift of life, for the breath in my lungs, and for the opportunities that lie ahead.

Guide my steps today, Lord. Help me to walk in Your ways and to be a light to those around me. Give me strength to face any challenges that may come, and wisdom to make decisions that honor You.

May my words be kind, my actions be just, and my heart be filled with love. Help me to see the good in others and to respond with compassion and understanding.

Bless my family, my friends, and all those I will encounter today. Keep them safe and shower them with Your love. Use me as an instrument of Your peace wherever I go.

I surrender this day to You, trusting in Your perfect plan for my life. Thank You for Your unfailing love and constant presence. Amen.
